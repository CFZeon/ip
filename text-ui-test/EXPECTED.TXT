Here are a list of available commands:
"done [number on list]": marks a task on the list as done
"todo [action]": adds a todo into the list
"deadline [action] /[limit] [date] [time]": adds a deadline into the list
deadlines also print your own strings, or just dates alone
"event [action] /[limit] [date] [time]": adds an event into the list
events also print your own strings, or just dates alone
"help": brings you to this menu!
"delete [number on list]": removes a task from the list
"bye": exits duke
Failed to use date and time properly? Try this:
dd/MM/yyyy HHmm
